Principal's Message


Dear Parents and all Well-Wishers, I am highly pleased to write this note in the capacity of the Principal of S.C.B.K ATAL UTKRISHT GOVT INTER COLLEGE. At the outset, I profusely thank all the faculty members, students, and other staff of the Institute for their continuous efforts and endeavors to take the institute to better levels year after year. The institute has not been established just to make the students obtain degrees alone.

Its motto is to help the students cultivate self-discipline, a learning attitude, and self-confidence. We are committed to the success of the students through holistic education with personality development. The objective of our institution is to empower the students so that they can become excellent professionals. So we focus on innovative teaching and learning processes with high moral values so that they can meet the demands of the global engineering sector. Our institute strives hard to give the students both theoretical as well as practical knowledge with deep perception so that they can face any challenge in their professions at national or international levels.

School Facilities

Science Lab

Our esteemed government school takes immense pride in providing students with access to a cutting-edge science laboratory. Equipped with modern apparatus and advanced resources, our lab offers a dynamic learning environment for hands-on scientific exploration.

Computer Lab

With cutting-edge computer facilities at our government school, students gain access to a wide range of educational resources and online learning platforms. The integration of technology in our classrooms not only enhances traditional teaching methods but also opens doors to a world , enabling students to expand their horizons and embrace the limitless possibilities of the digital realm.

Sports

With a strong emphasis on sportsmanship and healthy competition, our government school cultivates a thriving sporting culture. Through regular inter-school competitions, sports festivals, and training camps, our students have the chance to showcase their skills, build camaraderie, and learn invaluable life lessons such as discipline.
